Our Story

Let us introduce ourselves: we are passionate filmmakers who never imagined we'd be creating wedding films. We started out working on big projects, traveling around the world to create documentaries and commercials.

When we shot our first wedding, we instantly knew this was something special. But we wanted to do it differently. The slow pace, dramatic tone, and overly formal atmosphere needed to be reimagined. We wanted something bold. Something fresh, yet timeless and nostalgic.

Our style is experimental. We use a mix of cameras to capture different perspectives. We love shooting atypical scenes, and sound design plays a huge role, too. After the wedding, we compose your love story because storytelling is so much more than just a collection of beautiful shots.

What fuels us most is working with you. Being part of your love journey is an honor, and we're more than eager to craft memories that will last a lifetime.
