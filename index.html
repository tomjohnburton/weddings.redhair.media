Our Story

Redhair Media started when I filmed my best friend's wedding. I wasn't planning to — but standing behind the camera that day, I realised how powerful it is to capture something real, something unrepeatable.

Since then, I've focused on creating films that feel honest and cinematic — not staged, not over-produced. I look for the moments that tell the story quietly: the glance before walking down the aisle, the sound of laughter after the speeches, the calm between the chaos.

Every couple has their own rhythm. My job is simply to notice it — and to make something that will still feel true years from now.
